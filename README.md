## 🔍 What I actually do

**Diagnose** · Interview key staff, map real workflows, identify where and why adoption is stalling — not where leadership thinks it's stalling.

**Strategize** · Build the roadmap with senior leadership. Align AI adoption to business outcomes, not tool features.

**Embed** · Work directly with managers and ICs to build habits, not just awareness. Coach the behavior changes that make AI stick.

**Build infrastructure** · Prompt libraries, playbooks, governance frameworks, onboarding resources — the scaffolding that means the work outlasts the engagement.
